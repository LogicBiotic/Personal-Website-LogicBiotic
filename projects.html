Buwen Bot — A Filipino Gacha Experience

Buwen is a custom-built Discord gacha bot designed to capture the thrill of random rewards while integrating Filipino cultural elements. Players can roll daily to collect virtual cards of varying rarities and themes. Future updates will add upgrades to extend playtime, encourage frequent log-ins, and enhance the overall experience.

Developed with discord.py and planned to use Firebase (Basic Plan) for secure and scalable user data management, Buwen includes features like cooldown tracking, progress saving, and a constantly expanding pool of rewards.
