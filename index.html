Field & Lab Work: Trait-Based Ecology Research

As part of my BS Biology (Ecology and Evolutionary Biology Track) degree — graduated with Latin honors — I trained in scientific writing, research design, data wrangling, field collection, and laboratory protocol, with a focus on benthic macroinvertebrate ecology.

This work culminated in my undergraduate thesis “Spatio-temporal Variations of the Functional Traits of Benthic Macroinvertebrates in the University of the Philippines Diliman Campus Waterways,” which utilized trait-based approaches to assess freshwater ecosystem health across time and space.

Creative Manager: BlueSpark Solutions Philippines x Enterogermina Vietnam

At BSP, I oversaw creative operations for a brand campaign held at Vạn Hạnh Mall in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam for the months of January to March. I worked closely with Vietnamese clients (via English) and coordinated internal teams to manage timelines, organize deliverables, and present updates effectively.

I also co-developed the PR kit—contributing to both 2D designs (posters, digital art cards) and 3D elements (physical kit structure and packaging) for Sanofi’s Enterogermina Vietnam.

Buwen: Python-Based Gacha Bot on Discord

A work-in-progress Discord bot that simulates daily gacha pulls using a custom database of collectible characters. Built in Python, Buwen is designed with modularity in mind, allowing for daily roll limits, rarity-based drop rates, and user inventories. This ongoing personal project reflects my interest in game systems and backend logic, and is part of a larger goal to explore Discord API integration and gamified user interaction.
